Who Are We?

Body by Design is a studio dedicated to the influence of design + movement + wellness. It is a hybrid model, serving commercial constituents while promoting community engagement. So potential clients looking to incorporate a wellness practice into their daily routine, inspired by principles of both movement + design. We seek to elevate the quality of peoples' lives through introducing this concept on both the private and public levels.
